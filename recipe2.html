Seven Layered Dip

    Ingredients

  • 1 can: Refried Beans
  • Olives
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Guac & lemon
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Green onions
  • Sour cream
  • Taco seasoning
  • Cilantro
  • Green onions
  • Garlic salt
  • Black pepper

    Directions

  1. Bottom layer: Cooked refried beans
  2. Layer: Olives
  3. Layer: Sliced tomatoes
  4. Layer: Guac & lemon
  5. Layer: Cheddar cheese
  6. Layer: Green onions
  7. Layer: Mix of sour cream, taco seasoning, cilantro garlic salt, black pepper
