Grilled Chicken & Salad

    Ingredients

  • 2-3 lb: Chicken Breasts
  • 1: Lemon
  • 2 Tsp: Olive Oil
  • 3: Garlic Cloves
  • 2 tsp: Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp: Salt
  • 2-3 lb: Black Pepper
  • 1/2 tsp: Chicken Breasts

    Directions

  1. Cut chicken breasts
  2. Marinate chicken w/ lemon, oil, garlic, oregano, salt black pepper for 30 min
  3. Preheat grill on medium-high
  4. Grease pan w/ oil
  5. Place chicken on grill
  6. Close lid and grill for 8-10 min turn half-way
  7. Remove from grill, cover w/ foil, let rest for 5 min
